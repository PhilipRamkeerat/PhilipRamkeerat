## About me

I'm a software developer with a Computer Science degree and professional experience dating back to 2016. My work is centered on modern frontend engineering, with deep experience across the Angular ecosystem, component architecture, state management, and frontend performance.

I enjoy designing frontend architecture, simplifying complex systems, improving developer experience, and building products that are fast, inclusive, and easy to evolve.

- 🅰️ Specialized in **Angular, TypeScript, RxJS, and NgRx**
- 🧩 Experienced with **design systems, micro-frontends, and modular architecture**
- 🧪 Focused on quality through **unit, integration, and end-to-end testing**
- ♿ Committed to **accessibility, performance, and responsive design**
- 🤖 Using AI-assisted workflows to research, prototype, review, and ship more effectively
- 🌱 Always learning and refining how software is designed, built, and maintained

I use AI as an engineering multiplier—not a replacement for sound judgment. My workflow includes:

- **GitHub Copilot and Copilot CLI** for implementation, exploration, and repetitive tasks
- **Claude Code** for codebase analysis, planning, and complex refactoring
- **Cursor** for AI-assisted editing and rapid iteration
